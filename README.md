🛒 Flipkart E-commerce Data Analysis (EDA)
📌 Project Overview

This project performs an in-depth Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a Flipkart e-commerce dataset containing 20,002 product records. The dataset includes product details such as pricing, categories, brands, ratings, and descriptions.

The goal is to extract meaningful insights about pricing strategies, discounts, and customer behavior to help businesses make better decisions.

💡 Key Insights
Majority of products are in the mid-price range
Discounts alone do not improve product ratings
Product quality matters more than pricing
Strong correlation between retail and discounted prices
Some categories are highly competitive
📊 Business Recommendations
Focus on mid-range pricing strategy
Improve product quality to increase ratings
Use discounts strategically, not excessively
Promote top-performing categories and brands
✅ Conclusion

Pricing, category, and product quality significantly influence performance. While discounts attract customers, they are not enough to ensure success. A balanced approach combining quality, pricing, and customer satisfaction is key.
